Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Mini UPS.

  • How do I track my package?
    You can track your package in real time by logging into your account and clicking "Track a package" in the navigation bar.
  • What delivery options are available?
    We offer Basic (3-5 days), Express (1-2 days), and Premium (same-day, selected areas) delivery plans. See the Pricing page for details.
  • How can I change my delivery address?
    If your package is still in "delivering" status, you can edit the destination address from the package details page.
  • What should I do if my package is lost?
    Please contact our customer support immediately. If you purchased insurance, you may be eligible for compensation.
  • How do I contact customer support?
    You can reach us via the Support link in the footer or email us at support@miniups.com.
